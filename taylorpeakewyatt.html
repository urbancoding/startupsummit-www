Taylor Peake Wyatt

Co-Founder and CEO of MotionMobs

Taylor Peake Wyatt is an entrepreneur technology consultant and - most recently - a real estate investor. She is the co-founder of MotionMobs and has been managing the company since its inception in 2010.

As a native of Birmingham, Taylor co-founded this interactive firm a close friend who is now consulting for the company in San Francisco. MotionMobs is a fast-growing technology group focused on bringing strategy and efficiency to a wide range of companies through mobile technology, intuitive user-experiences, and integrated software solutions.

Taylor has also served as a leader on non-profit boards for State Farm and Coca-Cola – overseeing grant funds awarded to organizations throughout the country. She is also a part of the Coca-Cola Scholar alumni network.
