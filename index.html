About

Join us as founders from the local startup community share their stories and answer questions. Seats are limited and reserved for those whom we believe have the most to gain from attending. The Startup Drinks mixer will immediately follow the event!

Purpose

A free, one-day event featuring talks by local startup founders. Our goal is to inspire tomorrow's founders of high-growth companies in Birmingham. Come be inspired to start something by interacting with those whom already have.
