Startup Summit

Birmingham 2013

Join local startup founders for a free day of talks inspiring the next generation of high-growth companies in Birmingham.

About

Join us as founders from the local startup community share their stories and answer questions. Seats are limited and reserved for those who we believe have the most to gain from attending. The Startup Drinks mixer will immediately follow the event!

Location

Startup Summit will continue its residency at Birmingham's best event hall, Workplay, on April 6, 2013 at 8:00 am:

500 23rd St S
Birmingham, AL 35233

History

Startup Summit was started last year as an outreach of Birmingham's monthly Startup Drinks event to inspire new founders and strengthen our entrepreneurial community. In our second year, we hope to build on last year's tremendous success.
